Guaranteed Access Program (GAP)

What is the Guaranteed Access Program?

The Guaranteed Access Program (GAP) provides a one-time, tax-free payment to a person's loved ones at the time of their passing. It is intended to help cover final expenses during a very difficult time for a person's loved ones.

Key Information

  • Eligibility: Available to individuals under the age of 80 who are living with a long-term medical condition.
  • Guaranteed access: No medical tests or medical records are required to qualify.
  • Benefit payment: A tax-free lump sum (minimum $10,000) is paid to a person of your choosing at the time of your passing.
  • Maturity benefit: If you live to the end of the term, the funds are paid directly to you instead. Minimum payout is $10,000.
  • Guaranteed payout: The funds are always paid out — either to a loved one upon passing, or to you if you outlive the term.
  • Monthly contribution: The program is designed to be financially accessible for people at all income levels. Cost is typically $1 to $2 a day and is guaranteed to never increase.

Speak with a Benefits Specialist

To learn more about the Guaranteed Access Program and apply, you'll speak with a Benefits Specialist. They'll explain how the program works and the benefits it includes, answer your questions, and assist you with applying for additional disability benefits programs.

The Benefits Specialist will also assist you with (if applicable):

  • Canada Disability Benefit
  • Canadian Dental Care Plan
  • Canada Pension Plan Disability
  • Registered Disability Savings Plan
  • Disability Tax Credit Certificate

Note: Schedule a Benefits Review session with a Benefits Specialist only if you intend on attending.
